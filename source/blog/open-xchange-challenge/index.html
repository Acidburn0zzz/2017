Ind.ie Friday Challenge from Open-Xchange

21st November, 2014 — Aral Balkan

Thank you! We unlocked the Friday Challenge and got the $10,000 donation from Open-Xchange!

Rafael Laguna, CEO of Open-Xchange, has issued us with a Friday challenge: If we can get to $65,000 in donations by end of today, Open-Xchange will donate $10,000 to the http://Ind.ie crowdfunding campaign, bringing our total to $75,000.

If you haven’t had a chance to donate, now’s the time:

https://ind.ie

Even if you cannot donate, please share this with your friends. Your support today can trigger Open-Xchange’s $10,000 donation and give us a big push towards our goal.

About Rafael and Open-Xchange

I got the chance to spend time both on stage and off with Rafael in Munich recently, during the Open-Xchange Summit, where I presented the opening keynote I, Simulation.

In addition to running a successful open source business, Rafael is a fellow campainger for privacy and human rights. Read his recent post on http://Wired.com: Battling the Datenkraken’s Business of Surveillance.

Open-Xchange is a privately-owned company headquartered in Nuremberg, Germany. Their flagship product, OX App Suite is white-labeled software that challenges the status quo of messaging and collaboration software by empowering service providers to take back their customer relationship.
