Internet As A Commons

30th September, 2015 — Laura Kalbag

Tomorrow (Thursday 1st October) Aral is speaking on a panel at the European Parliament in Brussels. The conference is focused on “Internet as a Commons: Public Space in the Digital Age,” and Aral is taking part in “The Big Picture” panel:

What, if anything, in the current model of Internet Governance is clashing with a decentralized, resilient internet viewed as a common good? And what steps should be made by policymakers to foster the best environment for decentralized, community managed projects to grow?

The panel features Aral alongside Renata Avila, Jennifer Baker and Nicole Dewandre, and you can watch it live streamed at 15:30 CEST on the live event page.
