Foundation

We want to support individuals and organisations that have adopted the ind.ie manifesto and are working to create indie products. We also want to raise awareness about our social mission, to run educational campaigns, and to be a resource and voice to lead the conversation on the impact of technology on privacy, civil liberties, and democracy. We believe that a foundation is the best way to do this and we are in the exploratory stages of this initiative.

The aim of the ind.ie Foundation will be to support ind.ie phone as well as other indie initiatives.

If you have experience in this area and want to help out, please get in touch with us.
