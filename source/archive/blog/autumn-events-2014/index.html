Talks from the ind.ie team this autumn

16th September, 2014 — Laura Kalbag

This is just a quick post to let you know about upcoming events where you can hear us speak on ind.ie-related topics.

Recent events

Last week, Aral presented the Huxley debate at the British Science Festival.

The week before, Aral was in Istanbul as part of a panel on “Privacy as Innovation” for the UN-organised Internet Governance Forum (IGF). You can read Aral's thoughts on the panel on his site. Aral then gave his Free Is A Lie talk at the Internet Ungovernance Forum, which was held in Istanbul, parallel to the IGF.
