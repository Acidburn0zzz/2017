Ind.ie

We are a tiny social enterprise designing ethical technology that respects human rights, effort, and experience. Whenever we can, we also try to raise awareness of the issues and advise companies, policy makers, and educators through our talks, consulting, and events.

We’re based in Media Evolution City in Malmö, Sweden. We moved away from the UK and Cameron’s data-grabbing clutches.

Ind.ie (Article 12) is a UK company limited by guarantee, which means that we have no share capital, we can’t take equity investment/VC, and we cannot sell out to Google or Facebook if they ever offer us a billion dollars.

Meet the team and find out more by watching Decentralise Everything. You can hire Aral and Laura for consulting and speaking on privacy, ethical design, and accessibility.

Ind.ie is part of the WebWeWant coalition, and part of DataEthics.eu’s International Core Team.
