About

We are a tiny social enterprise building a platform to protect human rights and democracy in the digital age. Whenever we can, we also try to raise awareness of the issues and advise companies, policy makers, and educators through our talks, consulting, and events.

We’re currently based in the UK (Aral and Laura are working out of a garden shed in Reigate, and Jo is in Brighton) but we’re looking for a new home safe from Cameron’s data-grabbing clutches.

Ind.ie (Article 12) is a company limited by guarantee, which means that we have no share capital, we can’t take equity investment/VC, and we cannot sell out to Google or Facebook if they ever offer us a billion dollars.

Meet the team and find out more by watching The Camera Panopticon. You can hire Aral and Laura for consulting and speaking on privacy, ethical design, and accessibility.
