Ethical Design Manifesto 2015

Sell products, not people.

Make products that…

  1. Respect human rights

    Decentralised, private, open, interoperable, accessible, secure & sustainable.

  2. Respect human effort

    Functional, convenient & reliable

  3. Respect human experience

    Delight

Design, don’t decorate.

Design without ethics is decoration.

Decoration makes the status quo palatable, design alters it.

Be diverse, not ethnographic.

Design without diversity is ethnography.

A diverse team designing for themselves will meet the needs of a diverse audience.

Diversity is not altruism, it is competitive advantage. You cannot compete with a competent design team designing for themselves if you are designing for The Other.

Design the organisation, the product will follow.

Ethical Design is holistic or it is nothing.

Ethical Design is not what ethical designers do, it is the system of values and processes at the heart of ethical organisations. It begins with the design of the organisation itself.

Design your organisations so that your core values are respect for human rights, human effort, and human experience.
